# Topic 2 Exercises

Use `exercises/day2-research-pipeline.md` as the canonical Day 2 exercise path.

Focus on:

- research design memo;
- literature map;
- data or model-input map;
- issue-sized acceptance criteria;
- the Card-Krueger toy example in `examples/card-krueger-toy/`.

Minimum evidence:

- `docs/research_design_memo.md` separates claims from mechanics;
- `docs/data_source_map.md` documents source, units, transformations, and
  sample restrictions;
- issues name allowed files and verification checks;
- raw data are not modified.
